HOW TO PLAY — TACTICAL REFERENCE
OVERVIEW
Real-time 1v1 starship combat. Choose a vessel, pick one station to command — the computer auto-runs the rest. Fight until the enemy is destroyed, you escape via emergency warp, or your hull reaches zero.

Victory = enemy hull → 0  |  Escape = emergency warp at ≤35% hull  |  Defeat = your hull → 0

STATIONS
TACTICAL — You fire weapons & manage shields. Engineering auto-runs.
Best for: action-focused play, precise targeting, cloak timing.

ENGINEERING — You route power & dispatch repairs. Tactical auto-fires every 2.4s.
Best for: resource management, keeping systems alive under sustained fire.

HELM — You control speed, attack vector, range & manoeuvres. Both tactical & engineering run on auto.
Best for: strategic positioning, evasive play, setting up manoeuvre combos.

CAPTAIN'S CHAIR ⭐ — All three stations fully automated. You issue orders via crew: Worf (weapons), O'Brien/La Forge (engineering), Nog/Data (helm). 40+ order buttons.
Best for: experienced players who want tactical command without hands-on systems.
WEAPONS & ARCS
All weapons have firing arcs — buttons grey out when the arc doesn't cover your attack vector.

Energy weapons (cannons/phasers): Charge from EPS power. Higher power = faster recharge. Damage scales with targeting lock %.

Quantum torpedoes: Binary — ≥60% lock = full yield, <60% = ~55%. Both launchers per bay fire simultaneously.

Photon torpedoes: Flat damage, no lock required. Reliable at any time.

Range modifiers: Torpedoes +15% at long / −10% close. Cannons +20% close / −10% long.

⚠ Overload modes add large damage bonuses but risk breaker trips or misfire. Use them in decisive windows.
SHIELDS & COMBAT
Four shield sectors: Fore / Port / Starboard / Aft. Each absorbs hits independently.

Attack Vector — sets which shield sector the enemy is most likely (65%) to hit. Point your strongest sector at the enemy.

Reinforce — pumps energy from other sectors into one. Good before a Klingon charge or Romulan decloak.

Equalise — spreads shields evenly across all sectors (2s delay, shields dip to 80%).

Regen — shields recover automatically over time. Regen pauses for 3s after any hit. Higher shield power allocation → faster regen.

Lock % — your targeting lock builds over time. Higher lock = more energy weapon damage. Torpedoes need ≥60% for full yield.
ADVANCED MECHANICS
Deep Scan — reveals permanent frequency bonuses: shield freq (−25% dmg), hull fissure (+20% yield), sensor blind (−40% enemy lock), weapons disrupt (+30% fire interval). Run early — bonuses persist all fight.

Subsystem Targeting — focus on a specific enemy system. Deals 62% system damage + collateral hull bleed (~12–22 hull). Destroying cloak forces decloak. Destroying weapons cuts their fire.

Cloaking (Defiant) — 1200ms vulnerability window on engage/disengage. Shields accumulate regen credit while cloaked — decloak restores them. 25s cooldown, 4%/s power drain.

Antiproton Deflector (Enterprise) — toggle: charge the main deflector as a defensive screen. Incoming fire −35%, enemy targeting lock −50%. Weapon capacitors recharge ~45% slower while engaged (deflector draws EPS priority). Pairs with regenerative shields for a Borg-survival tank posture.

EPS Heat — sustained rapid fire heats conduits. Above 70% reduces capacitor recharge up to 30%. Cools passively at 8/s.

Ablative Armour (Defiant) — 6 layers, each absorbs 60% of a hull hit. Auto-regenerates after 45s per layer.
ENEMY ROSTER
Jem'Hadar — Polaron bypass (30% of damage skips shields). Gets faster as it takes damage. Rams at <20% hull (4s warning).
Cardassian — Fast lock rate, constant harassment fire.
Romulan BoP/Warbird — Cloak + sensor ghosts. Fires plasma torpedo immediately on decloak. Cloaks again at 25% hull.
Klingon K'Tinga/Vor'Cha — Closes to brawl range (+40% disruptor damage close). Death salvo at 10% hull.
Borg Probe — Adapts per weapon type (+2.4%/hit, cap 65%). Rotate weapons constantly. Use Tricobalt (no adaptation). Tractor beam.
KEY TIPS
🔭 Scan early — run Deep Scan in the first 10s. Disrupt scan (+30% fire interval) is often the strongest opener.
🛡 Attack vector — always point your strongest shield at the enemy. Fore is usually best (highest capacity).
⚡ Burst/Concentrated fire — use at ≥20% lock to overwhelm shield regen before it recovers.
📍 Klingons — stay long range. Their disruptors gain +40% at close quarters.
👻 Romulan ghosts — hold fire when ghosts appear. Wait for the real decloak, then punish the 1.5s vulnerability window.
🔴 Jem'Hadar ram — pump fore shields and activate Evasive Pattern when you see the ramming countdown.
🤖 Borg — never fire the same weapon twice in a row. Rotate: cannons → nose → torpedoes → cannons. Save Tricobalt — it ignores adaptation.
⚡ Last Stand — below 20% hull, the crew acts automatically: full impulse, shield boost. Use this window for overloads.
